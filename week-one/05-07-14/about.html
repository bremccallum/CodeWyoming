More About Me

What services do I provide again?
Print design and web design including logos, branding, packaging, front end web design. I also love to spend as much time in my sketchbook as I can. Drawing lettering is one of my favorite things to sketch!
Where did I come from?
I grew up in Riverton, WY where I lived until college when I moved to Colorado. After college and a year of teaching ski school in Crested Butte, I came back to Wyoming to live in Jackson Hole.
What is my education?
I recieved a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design with an Emphasis in Print from Colorado Mesa University. I also have a minor in studio art.
What are my other interests?
As I said I love to draw, but I also love to snowboard, trail run, & hang out with my doggie.
